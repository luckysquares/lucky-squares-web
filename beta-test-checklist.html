Log out

Save as draft — appears in dashboard
Edit draft — fields update correctly
Launch campaign — Stripe checkout opens for platform fee
Pay platform fee with test card
Campaign status changes to active after payment
Campaign page /f/[id] publicly accessible after launch

4. Buyer: purchasing squares

Stripe checkout opens and completes
Buyer confirmation email arrives with square numbers
Purchased squares show as sold on grid
First-sale notification arrives at organiser email
Buying an already-sold square is blocked

Poster page (/f/[id]/poster) loads
Claim prize page (/claim-prize/[token]) loads and records claim
